## **Recurrent Neural Networks VS Transformers**

### **What is a Recurrent Neural Network?**
It is a neural network architecture specialized in processing sequential data with recurrent connections.
*   **Recurrent Connections**: They utilize recurrent connections.
*   **Sequential Processing**: They process data sequentially.

---

### **What is a Transformer?**
It is an architecture that learns the context and meaning of data by analyzing relationships in sequential data, using attention mechanisms.
*   **Attention Mechanisms**: They utilize attention mechanisms and positional embedding.
*   **Parallel Processing**: They process data in parallel.
